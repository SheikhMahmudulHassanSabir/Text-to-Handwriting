Guide to Use

Get the most out of the Text to Handwriting converter by following this simple guide. Transform your typed words into authentic-looking handwriting in just a few steps.

Step 1: Enter Your Text

Paste or type your content into the "Your Text" area on the home page. The preview will update instantly as you type.

Step 2: Choose Your Style

Select from a variety of handwriting fonts. Each font has its own character, from formal script to casual notes. You can also change the ink color between black, blue, and red.

Step 3: Refine the Layout

  • Font Size: Adjust to fit your desired scale.
  • Line Height: Increase for an airy feel or decrease for compact notes.
  • Word & Letter Spacing: Fine-tune these to make the handwriting look more natural.
  • Margins: Set the white space around your text.

Step 4: Apply Effects

Use the "Visual Effect" dropdown to add realism. The Scanner effect adds subtle grit, while Paper Texture simulates real paper fibers.

Step 5: Export

Once you're happy with the results, download your creation as a high-resolution PNG image or a multi-page PDF document.

Pro Tip

For the most realistic look, use a larger font size and slightly increase the letter spacing (around 2px-4px). This mimics the natural flow of a pen.
